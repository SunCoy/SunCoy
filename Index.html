About SunCoy

SunCoy is an eco-friendly blockchain network powered by solar energy. We are committed to reducing the environmental impact of cryptocurrencies by utilizing renewable energy sources and energy-efficient algorithms.

Key Features

  • Solar-powered blockchain nodes
  • Low-energy consensus mechanism
  • Carbon offset partnerships
  • Eco-friendly NFTs
  • Incentives for renewable energy usage
